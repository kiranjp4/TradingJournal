# Day 3 — ASIL and safety goals (introduction)

**Read:** ISO 26262-1 — ASIL, safety goal, safe state, safety-related function  
**Also skim:** ISO 26262-3, Clause 6.4 (Safety goals) — overview only  
**Time:** 35–45 min  

---

## Step 1: Read (15 min)

Focus on:

- What ASIL represents (risk classification, not "quality level").
- What a safety goal must contain.
- What a "safe state" means.
