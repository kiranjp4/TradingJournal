# Day 4 — Safety lifecycle

**Read:** ISO 26262-2, Clause 6 — overview of lifecycle phases and key work products  
**Time:** 30–40 min  

---

## Step 1: Read (15 min)

Identify the main phases: concept, system, hardware, software, production, operation, service, decommissioning.

---

## Step 2: Practical exercise (20 min)

Create `notes/26262/part-02/lifecycle.md`:

1. List lifecycle phases in order.
2. For each phase, note **one work product** (e.g., HARA, safety case, SW unit test report).
3. Mark where your current job mostly sits.

Optional mermaid:

```mermaid
flowchart LR
  Concept --> System --> HW --> SW --> Prod --> Ops
```

### Reflection

Answer in 3 sentences: Where would a defect caught "too late" cost the most?
